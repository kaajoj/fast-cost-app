# Privacy Policy - FastCost

**Last Updated: May 5, 2026**

## Introduction
Thank you for using **FastCost**. This Privacy Policy explains how FastCost handles information. 

**The app does not collect, transmit, or share personal data with the developer or any third parties. 

It operates fully offline and does not require any personal permissions on the device.**

## No Data Collection
Any information entered in the app, such as expenses, is stored **only locally on the device** in the app's private storage. This data is not collected, transmitted, or shared.

The app does not track, store, or transmit identifying information such as:
- IP addresses or device identifiers
- Email addresses or contact information
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral tracking

## Advertising
FastCost does not display advertisements and does not use advertising identifiers.

## Third-Party Services
FastCost does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools that collect or process user data.

## No Required Permissions
FastCost does not request or require any sensitive permissions on the device. It does not access the camera, microphone, location, contacts, or external storage. 

The app is designed to operate fully offline and does not transmit data to external servers or third parties.

## Data Storage & Backups
All expense data lives exclusively in a local SQLite database stored in the app's private internal storage.
- **Uninstallation:** If the app is uninstalled, all locally stored data is removed along with it.
- **Android Auto Backup:** If Android Auto Backup is enabled on the device, the operating system may include the app's local database in the user's personal Google account backup. This process is controlled entirely by device settings and Google; FastCost does not have access to this backup data.

## Security
Since FastCost does not collect or transmit any data, the risks associated with data transmission and remote server breaches are minimized. The security of data relies on the device's own protection measures, such as:
- Screen lock (PIN, password, or biometrics)
- Operating system sandboxing
- Device-level encryption

## User Rights
As no personal data is collected or stored externally, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete from any remote systems. Full control over information is maintained by the user directly on the device.

## Children's Privacy
FastCost is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from children. No information is collected from any users, as the app is designed to function without any data collection capabilities.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date. Regular review of this policy is encouraged to stay informed about privacy practices.
